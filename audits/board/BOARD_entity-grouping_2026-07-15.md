   same evidence (all six). Canonical row shows the canonical key's OWN score; never max()
   (the "mcgregor 94.4 > conor_mcgregor 93.3" alias-shopping trap — named by five memos).
2. **Aliases are dated, versioned, evidence-stamped, confidence-scored, human-confirmed
   (flag-never-force), reversible** — identity is temporal ("conor" can change referent).
3. **DB-persisted + warm-on-boot from day one** (CATEGORY_SNAPSHOT clone) — do not replay
   the 07-11 warm/cold incident on list counts; writer-guard any grouped-count metric.
4. **Held-out wall enforced in code** — the alias map must never be importable into scoring
   admission, calibration, ledger enrollment, or the sweep.
5. **Ledger stays per-key everywhere including UI** — no family-best verdicts; per-key
   detection dates ARE the falsifiability.
6. **Detail view shows de-duplicated evidence** — union of distinct raw_signals ("N
   independent articles across the entity"), never a cross-key mention sum; label that
   constituents share underlying evidence.
7. **3-platform parity + /categories consistency in the same change; auditors keep
   measuring RAW keys** (grouping must not shrink monitoring denominators).
